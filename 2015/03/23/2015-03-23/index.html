State College in Winter

2015-03-23 Photography Views

In fact, this post should not be named as State College in Winter since these photos below I shot was not even beyond 500 meters in the vicinity of my apartment where I am living in State College, Pennsylvania. But, it is too shame that I have not brought my camera to take some photos in the downtown of State College even I just need to drive a couple of minutes. For me, as a being away academic environment over almost two years poor graduated student, I was struggling with my first term in United States. Thankfully, I have been through this term and now it is summer time. This summer, I still have many tasks I expect I can get them done. Firstly, I am currently studying Python which I want to apply it to help my future research and doing some small programming to make my life exciting. Secondly, I have to start to prepare my PhD candidacy exam which will obviously become my top priority and the biggest challenge. I just hope I can pass it this time without any further pain. Thirdly, I should start to read and make notes on those published papers which cover the research areas in nano scale composite materials and also structure vibration.

Words have been typed so many but where photos are as mentioned in the beginning.

Photos always follow behind them.


In this photo, you can find my apartment window.


This is photo I obviously applied diagonal composition and two lovely bright areas on the red brick wall of my living apartment.


The reason why I post this photo is that I found this photo reflects so much details on this tree and very clear like the imagine shot mid-frame camera. This photo, I remembered, was shot by my Leica Summarit-M 75mm f/2.4 lens. Honestly, I did not use this lens too much in daily because I do not know what I should shoot for by using this lens except people portraits. I think this photo approves its high quality. It is my incapability can not exert its capability.
